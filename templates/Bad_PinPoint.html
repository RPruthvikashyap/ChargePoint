Bad PinPoint Diagnostic

Bad PinPoint

A "Bad PinPoint" refers to an incorrect geographical location displayed for the station on the map within the Network Operating System (NOS) Prop Sheet. This means the station's pinpoint is not accurately reflecting its physical location.

Step 1: Login to NOS and Access Configuration

  • Log in to NOS and pull up the station prop-sheet.
  • Go to the **Configuration** tab.
  • Click on the **Edit** button.
Step 1 of 3

Step 2: Drag the Pin to the Correct Location

  • In the MAP section where the wrong pinpoint is showing up, **drag the pin to the correct physical location** of the station.
  • Observe that the **Latitude** and **Longitude** values will automatically update as you drag the pin.
Step 2 of 3

Step 3: Save the Updated Configuration

  • Scroll down to the bottom of the Configuration page.
  • Click on the **Save** button to apply the changes.

The station's pinpoint should now be correctly updated in the system.
